### Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| Artificial Intelligence | Technology enabling machines to simulate human intelligence and perform cognitive tasks |
| Machine Learning | Subset of AI where systems learn from data without explicit programming |
| Deep Learning | ML technique using multi-layered neural networks for complex pattern recognition |
| Computer Vision | AI capability to interpret and analyze visual information from images/videos |
| Natural Language Processing | AI technology for understanding and generating human language |
| Generative AI | AI systems that create new content based on learned patterns |
| Anomaly Detection | Identifying data points that deviate significantly from normal patterns |
| Azure AI Services | Microsoft's cloud-based suite of AI tools and APIs |

### Memory Tricks
- **AI > ML > DL** - Think of Russian nesting dolls: AI is the biggest, ML fits inside it, and DL is the smallest inside ML
- **PACNG** for AI workloads: **P**rediction, **A**nomaly detection, **C**omputer vision, **N**LP, **G**enerative AI (remember: "PAC-MaN Games")
- **NLP = "Natural Language People"** - computers trying to understand how people naturally communicate
- **Anomaly = Abnormal** - both start with "A" and mean something unusual or out of place
